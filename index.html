Crystals Collector!

How to play

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game in the panel titled "Number to Match".

  2. There are four crystals. Blue, Green, Red and Yellow. By clicking on the Green and Yellow and by moving your mouseover the Blue and Red you will add a specific amount of points to your total score shown in the field "Your Number"

  3. You will win the game by matching your total score shown in the field "Your Number" to the random number, shown in the field "Number to match"or lose the game if your total score in the "Your Number" field goes above the number in the field "Number to match"

  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and figure how much it is worth by doing simple math in your head (or on your phone).

  5. Each time the game is restarted the values of each crystals and the value you have to guess is randomised

  6. The times you loose or win are recorded in the field named "Score"
